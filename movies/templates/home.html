Media-Mate

This NLP-based media recommendation project suggests movies, songs, and books using a combination of popularity-based, user-based, and collaborative filtering techniques. It analyzes user input, media descriptions, and reviews to recommend relevant content by identifying key features like genre and mood. Popularity-based filtering suggests the most widely appreciated content, user-based filtering personalizes recommendations based on individual preferences, and collaborative filtering finds patterns in similar users’ choices. The system delivers categorized recommendations through a Django interface, offering an engaging, data-driven experience for discovering media.

Content Based Filtering

Content-based filtering recommends items by analyzing their attributes and suggesting similar items based on what you’ve previously liked, ensuring a personalized experience tailored to your interests.

Popularity Based Filtering

Popularity-based filtering recommends items based on their overall popularity, highlighting the most liked, viewed, or purchased items to provide users with trending content.

Collaborative Filtering

Collaborative filtering recommends items based on the preferences and behaviors of similar users, leveraging patterns in user interactions to suggest content that others with similar tastes have enjoyed.

Hybrid Filtering

Hybrid filtering combines multiple recommendation approaches, such as popularity, content-based, and collaborative filtering, to provide more accurate and diverse suggestions by leveraging the strengths of each method.

NOTE:-The recommendation models in Media Mate are currently trained on a dataset up until 2014. Once we obtain the latest dataset, we will update the platform to enhance the recommendations with more up-to-date content and user preferences.
