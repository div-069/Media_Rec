Movie Recommendation System

A content-based movie recommendation system suggests movies to users by analyzing the features of the movies themselves, such as genre, cast, director, and plot.The system evaluates the attributes of movies that the user has watched or rated highly and finds other movies with similar characteristics. This approach helps users discover movies they may not have come across otherwise but are likely to enjoy based on their preferences. Platforms like Netflix use a blend of content-based and collaborative filtering methods to enhance their recommendations by considering both the content and behavior of users.
